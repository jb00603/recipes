Air Fried Oreos

by Lindsey Moore

Ingredients

  • 8 Oreo cookies ...
  • 1 package of pillsbury Crescent Rools
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Directions

1. spread out crescent dough onto a cutting board. 2. using your finger, press down into each perforated line so it forms on big sheet. 3. cut the dough into eighths. 4. Place Oreo cookie in center of each of the crescent roll squares and roll each corner up. 5. Bunch the rest fo the crescet roll to make sure it covers the entire oreo cookie. Do not stretch the crescent roll too thin or it will break. 6. preheat air fryer to 320 degreees for about 2-3 minutes. 7. Gently place the air fried Oreos inside the air fryer in one even row so they do not touch. If you have a smaller air fryer, cook in batches. 8. Cook Oreos on 320 degrees for 5-6 minutes until golden brown on the outside. 9. Carefully remonve the Air Fryer Oreos from the air fryer and immediately dust them with powdered sugar. 10. Let cool for two minutes then enjoy!
